Our project, "Virtual Graffiti: Laser Interaction on Architectural Canvases," explores the intersection of technology and art by leveraging lasers to project dynamic visuals onto buildings. The project aims to redefine urban artistic expression and provide a unique canvas for public engagement and creativity within open spaces.

As a team, we are committed to creating a portable, scalable, and visually impactful software solution. Our journey involves exploring augmented reality experiences, facilitating real-time audience engagement, and achieving responsive adjustments to the projector setup. We invite you to join us on this exciting exploration at the crossroads of technology and artistic expression.
